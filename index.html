Who We Are

Techrise Academy is a hands-on coding bootcamp built for aspiring developers. Founded with a mission to democratize tech education, we are Nigeria's growing community of JavaScript developers — and we believe coding is a skill anyone can master with the right environment, not just a degree or prior experience.

We don't just teach JavaScript — we build problem-solvers who can ship real products. From zero coding experience to building full-stack applications, our community supports every step with mentorship, peer collaboration, and project-based learning. Our intensive programs combine live instruction, real-world projects, and career support to transform beginners into job-ready developers.

Techrise Academy started with a simple idea: what if quality developer training was accessible, practical, and community-driven? Today, we're a thriving ecosystem where beginners become builders. Through our flagship JavaScript programs, students master modern frameworks, collaborate on real projects, and connect with mentors who've worked in the industry.
